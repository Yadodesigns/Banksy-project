BUY ON OPENSEA

Choose an event from the Explore Events or Current Events page. If the DOORTOKEN™ you selected is listed on OpenSea, there will be a ON OPENSEA link under the Purchase button for the DOORTOKEN™.

How do I Access The Event?

Once your DOORTOKEN™ is purchased, you will be prompted to register with our Discord Once in the Discord, you will connect your digital wallet to CollabLab in the Verify Channel. Next, activate your DOORTOKEN™ for event use by clicking the ACTIVATE DOORTOKEN™ button.

Now you are all set and ready to attend the event!

If your DOORTOKEN™ includes special private access, scheduling will be provided by our Discord Concierge.
